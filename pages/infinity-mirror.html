Infinity Mirror

My website is embeddable as an SVG, so you can do this:

Depending on your browser, the image might stop repeating at a certain depth. It'll also switch to the mobile view once it gets small enough.

If you want to embed my site, use one of the following HTML snippets:

<object type="image/svg+xml" width="800px" height="500px" data="https://brightshard.dev/index.svg"></object>
<img width="800px" height="500px" src="https://brightshard.dev/index.svg" />

You can set it to the infinity mirror page by adding #infinity-mirror to the end of the URL. You can also change the width and height properties to whatever you want.

The first snippet is interactable (you can click and navigate around the site). The second unfortunately is not.
