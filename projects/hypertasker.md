## What is Hypertasker?
Hypertasker is an open-source app for combining small tasks such as emails, notes, RSS, calendars, and to-do lists.

## What is the release schedule looking like?
My country is still having problems with COVID, so we are not in person at school. Online school is much more taxing than in-person and thus I won't be able to get much work done until we're in person or the school year releases. I hope to have a release by this summer (Probably June-July).

## Emails in Hypertasker
One of Hypertasker's main features will be an email client. Although at first there will be limited support for sending emails, you will be able to both read and send emails by the time Hypertasker comes out of Alpha.

## Note-taking in Hypertasker
I will also build in a note-taking section for Hypertasker. I will try my best to also put in MarkDown support by the first release, but that might not happen - it depends on if I can figure out how to get that working or not.

## Calendars and To-Dos in Hypertasker
I haven't built much into Hypertasker, but it can already get remote calendars, like Google Calendar or even Canvas.

## RSS in Hypertasker
To be honest, I'm not sure if I'll be able to implement an RSS reader. I will have to look into how to get that working. Once I do, though, Hypertasker will come with a full-fledged RSS news reader.

## Why am I making this?
My goal with Hypertasker is to combine lots of somewhat niche tasks into one app. Finding one service with calendars, to-dos, notes, emails, and other tasks in one place is difficult, if not impossible; so I'm doing it myself. I'm tired of opening 20 tabs for various emails and notes, so instead, I'll put it in one place - and in an app that I control, not in a greedy company's website.

## Can I run Hypertasker on Windows/MacOS/Linux/etc?
Yes. You can run Hypertasker on almost anything, actually. This is because I use [Beeware](https://beeware.org) in my apps. Beeware lets you make an app in Python and deploy it anywhere. You can get more information from the Beeware page in the sidebar!
